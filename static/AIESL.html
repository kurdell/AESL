About

Absolute Import & Export Services LLC (AIESL) is a subsidiary of its parent company, Absolute Engineering Services Limited (AESL) located in Trinidad, the Caribbean. This business is intended primarily to build the market of agricultural exports from Trinidad to the USA focusing on three (3) main market segments; Houston, New York and Miami. Each area identified to have a characteristic and significant Caribbean presence in its population ideal for the intended business concept of pepper imports from Trinidad (Pimento, Sweet and Hot pepper varieties) based on their food-culture. AIESL was conceptualized as a result of the economic hardships experienced during the Covid-19 Pandemic and the significant reduction in business revenue to AESL. Absolute Engineering was forced to pivot its operations for survival and considered the food industry a most viable market to penetrate post pandemic. Our penetration drive in Trinidad comprised agricultural crops, poultry and a bakery initiative. The continuing economic depression and scarcity of USD currency availability made this time optimal for our entry into the US Market.
