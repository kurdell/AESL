About Us

Absolute Engineering Services Limited was established in February 2000 with a strong focus on Project Management and Engineering Consultancy. Since then we have expanded the scope of our operations to include the provision of labor and services for Health and Safety, Asset Management, Mechanical & Electrical Maintenance and Water Management systems. AESL is committed to providing customers with the highest service and performance, based on continuous self evaluation of our operational philosophies and ongoing training towards the ultimate goal of continuous improvement to achieve Best in Class status. In this way AESL is determined to meet, satisfy and surpass the myriad needs and requirements of our clientele. This we consider to be pivotal in our success as we maintain transparency, honesty and integrity in all our business dealings. Our reputation speaks for itself.
